I know this is an 11month old post, but I thought I would reply anyway. SVT and VT are not the same thing. SVT starts in the upper part of the heart and apparently wont kill you. VT is in the bottom half and can cause sudden death if not stopped (either by itself or by CD). You donâ€™t die at exactly 30seconds. Death occurs if it continues and turns into VF (not the same as AF). I have read that this could be minutes, hours, days or weeks (some people have had VT without any symptoms). I have already read the VT is normally only seen in hearts that have had previous damage, or if you have LQTS. If VT is possible, I would be finding out where my closest defibrillator is located so I know where to go/send someone, and getting a mobile ECG like a ECG smartwatch or Kardia device to check what your feeling really is potentially VT.
