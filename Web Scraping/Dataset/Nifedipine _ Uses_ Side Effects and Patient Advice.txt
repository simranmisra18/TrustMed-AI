Nifedipine is given to treat high blood pressure (hypertension), or to help prevent angina chest pain. It is also prescribed in the treatment of Raynaud's phenomenon, which is a condition caused by poor circulation to the hands and feet. You will have been prescribed it for one of these reasons.
